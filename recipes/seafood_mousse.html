Seafood Mousse

This disgusting recipe is made to look like a fish.

Made from fish, depicting fish, just as the devil himself intended...

Ingredients

  • 1 envelope unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups fish (halibut, salmon or tuna), drained and finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon capers, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried crushed marjoram
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • Carrot strips and sliced olives (optional)

Directions

  1. Soften gelatin in cold water; add boiling water and stir until gelatin dissolves.
  2. Cool. Add mayonnaise, lime juice, onion, salt and pepper; mix well and chill until mixture begins to set.
  3. Fold in fish, capers, parsley, tarragon, marjoram, and whipped cream.
  4. Pour into an oiled fish mold and chill until firm.
  5. Unmold on serving platter and, if desired, garnish with carrot strips and stuffed olive slices.
