Ham and Banana Hollandaise

This recipe seems slightly less disgusting than the other two (at first glance).

While ham and pineapple are a good combination, does that logic follow similarly with ham and bananas??

Make this recipe to find the answer...

Ingredients

  • 6 medium bananas
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 6 thin slices boiled ham (about 1/2 lb)
  • 3 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 2 envelopes (1 1/4-oz size) hollandaise sauce mix
  • 1/4 cup light cream

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Lightly butter 2-quart, shallow baking dish.
  2. Peel bananas; sprinkle each with 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice, to prevent darkening.
  3. Spread ham slices with mustard. Wrap each banana in slice of ham. Arrange in single layer in casserole. Bake 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, make sauce: In small saucepan, combine sauce mix with 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, and cream.
  5. Heat, stirring, to boiling; pour over bananas.
  6. Bake 5 minutes longer, or until slightly golden. Nice with a green salad for brunch or lunch.

Ham, bananas and hollandaise? The mustard truly makes this dish revolting.
