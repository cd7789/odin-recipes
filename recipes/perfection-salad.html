"Perfection Salad"

This 20th century monstrosity was a common place in the world of church potlucks.

Dispite the name "Perfection Salad," this dish is FAR from perfection.

While Jello has been relegated to gelatinous desserts, savory aspics may someday make a comeback with your help.

A perfectly constructed aspic from hell...

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Boiling Water
  • 3 Oz Package of Lemon Jell-O Gelatin
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons of Lemon Juice or Vinegar
  • 1 Cup of Cold Water
  • 1 Cup of Finely Diced Celery
  • 1 Cup of Finely Shredded Cabbage
  • 1 Tablespoon of Dried Diced Onion
  • 2 Tablespoons of Chopped Pimento
  • Mayonnaise (amount your heart desires)

Directions

  1. Pour boiling water over jello in bowl.
  2. Stir until jello is disolved.
  3. Stir in vinegar, veggies, salt, and cold water.
  4. Pour into mold, chill until firm.
  5. Serve unmolded on chilled plate, topped with a generous dollop of mayonnaise.
  6. If preferred, use 1/2 cup celery and 1 cup grated carrot.
  7. You can use sugar-free jello for a diabetic/lowcarb version of Perfection Salad. Sometimes lemon jello is difficult to find - we've sometimes used lime instead.

Congratulations!

You just made the exact opposite of perfection. Enjoy with extra mayonnaise...
